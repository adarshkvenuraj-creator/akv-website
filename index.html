About

Grounded in evidence, shaped by patients

Dr. Adarsh K. Venuraj is a dedicated and highly skilled physician with a passion for providing exceptional care in the fields of Diabetes, Cardiology, and Infectious Diseases. With a strong academic foundation and extensive clinical experience, he is committed to improving the health and well‑being of his patients through evidence-based medicine and personalised treatment plans.

He graduated from the prestigious Government Medical College, Kozhikode, and pursued his MD in Internal Medicine from Government Medical College, Thrissur — further honing his expertise with a Diplomate of National Board (DNB) in Internal Medicine and a Fellowship in Clinical Endocrinology from the Royal College of Physicians.

Care Pathways

From clinic visit to critical care

Outpatient Diabetes Care

Managing the condition to maintain healthy blood sugar levels and prevent complications — regular monitoring, personalised meal planning, exercise recommendations, and medication management, including insulin or oral hypoglycemics.

ICU Care

Life-saving treatment for patients facing critical illness or severe injury, backed by state-of-the-art technology and a multidisciplinary team monitoring and stabilising patients 24/7.

In-Patient Care

Comprehensive, round-the-clock medical attention for extended hospitalisation — from admission to discharge, ensuring the highest standards of treatment, comfort, and recovery.
